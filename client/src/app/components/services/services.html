Services

As a non-profit organization dedicated to cancer care and holistic patient support within the Hampton Roads area, we offer three essential services to those with a cancer diagnosis or their family members. With an objective to ease the process and management of care, our team works daily to get you to and from appointments, to provide you with a community of understanding individuals, and to assure access to the necessary health equipment for living with a cancer diagnosis.

To request any of the services listed below, simply call our office at 1-757-440-7501 or contact Susan Lawler at slawler@leesfriends.org.

All of our services are free of charge.
